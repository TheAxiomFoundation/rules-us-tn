Policy Manual Number 125.020, October 1, 2025, section 3.d.ii.1.c:

Utility Allowance: The utility allowance under the Supplemental Nutrition
Assistance Program (SNAP) used in lieu of the community spouse's actual utility
expenses.

ii. The Basic Utility Allowance (BUA) is used when the community spouse is
responsible for at least two utility expenses, but is not responsible for
heating or cooling costs.
The BUA is $162, effective October 1, 2025.
